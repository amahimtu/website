About.

Hi, I’m a UX and product designer. I leverage principles and concepts from human-computer interaction and marketing to design experiences and products that are useful, usable, and desirable.

Before transitioning into design, I worked in the marketing divisions of world-renowned companies in the music and audio products industries, respectively. My UX skills, coupled with my marketing background have given me a holistic understanding of the design process within the larger business context.

As a musician, I am also passionate about music technology and performance, drums and percussion, and all things audio.

In May 2020, I received a Bachelor of Arts degree with distinction from the University of Michigan, where I double majored in communication & media, and music.
